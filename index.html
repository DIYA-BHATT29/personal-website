About   Me

Hello, I'm Diya Bhatt A, I am a B.Tech student specializing in Electronics and Computer Engineering from LBS Institute of Technology for Women, Poojapura, Trivandrum. Proficient in C and Python, I am also deeply interested in AI, cybersecurity, and cloud computing. I have attended various workshops, including a cybersecurity workshop from Techmaghi, as well as VLSI for Beginners, Python for Beginners, and MicroPython Primer from National Institute of Electronics and Information Technology Calicut (NIELIT).
In addition to my technical skills, which include photography videography, and video editing, I am a freelancing artist with two years of experience in selling mural works. My personal interests encompass drawing,painting(modern and mural)dancing (any style), music, forensics, and vintage art and culture.
I look forward to learning new tools and technologies and gaining new experiences. contact me. I'd love to hear from you!
